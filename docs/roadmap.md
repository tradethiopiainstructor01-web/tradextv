### TradeXTV Development Roadmap

#### Phase 1 – MVP (4–6 weeks)
- Auth with JWT + role-based access covering Viewer, Creator, Producer, Admin, Partner.
- Video upload pipeline with signed URLs plus VOD streaming playback.
- Editorial categories, shows, series, and featured videos.
- Admin dashboard for approvals, creators, categories, promotions, and analytics glimpse.
- Basic analytics (views, watch time, engagement) surfaced via the backend.

#### Phase 2 – Growth
- Live streaming (AWS IVS / Mux) and creator monetization tooling.
- Ads & promotions workflow with sponsor delivery and campaign reporting.
- Mobile-friendly experience (UI tweaks, responsive layouts).
- TradeEthiopia SSO integration for partners and B2B users.

#### Phase 3 – Advanced
- AI recommendations for personalized video queues and discovery.
- AI-driven captions, summaries, and multilingual support (Amharic/English).
- Smart business matchmaking blending TradeXTV programming with TradeEthiopia opportunities.

### Design Direction
- Dark, premium media theme with Netflix-style focus and clean business typography.
- Gold/blue accent palette to reinforce the TradeXTV brand positioning.
- Large thumbnails and dashboard-first layouts for both frontend heroes and admin controls.
- Creator studio + analytics views prioritize clarity, contrast, and quick actions.
